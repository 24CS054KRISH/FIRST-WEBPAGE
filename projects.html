Bluetooth Controlled Robotic Car (BEEE Project)

Semester: 1st Semester

This project was about building a robotic car controlled with an Android app via Bluetooth. We used Arduino Uno, L293D motor driver, and an HC-05 Bluetooth module.

Key Features: Wireless control, speed variation, obstacle movement.

Team Members: Krish Mendapara, Jugal Kshatriya, Aayush Malhotra, Rudra Patel, Parth Patel
